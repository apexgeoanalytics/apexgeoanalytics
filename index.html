Our Projects

Urban Planning
Riverscape Vision

Riverscape Vision is a pioneering project that utilizes geospatial technology to visualize future urban developments along riverfront areas. This initiative allows stakeholders to assess the visual and environmental impact of a proposed high-rise building, enhancing community involvement in sustainable urban planning.
Geo-Connect
Optimizing Cellular Coverage

GeoConnect is an innovative project that leverages Geographic Information Systems (GIS) technology to analyze mobile phone usage patterns in urban areas. By integrating spatial analysis techniques, we aim to propose optimal locations for new cell tower installations, enhancing connectivity and ensuring seamless access to data for all users.
Geospatial Environmental Assessment
GreenScape Insight

GreenScape Insight employs GIS technology to analyze urban greenery distribution. By mapping existing green spaces, we identify areas lacking vegetation, guiding municipal efforts to enhance urban ecology. This initiative aims to promote sustainable planning and improve community well-being by prioritizing greenery in under-served areas.
Corporate Resource Management
AssetOptimizer Pro

AssetOptimizer Pro utilizes web applications to streamline resource allocation and enhance productivity within corporate structures. By integrating data analytics and user-friendly interfaces, we provide tailored solutions that empower teams to efficiently manage assets, improve workflows, and drive overall organizational effectiveness.
Marine Geography
OceanEdge Mapping

OceanEdge Mapper employs advanced geospatial techniques to visualize oceanic boundaries and their relationship with continents. By creating detailed geographical maps, we help organizations understand the dynamics of ocean currents, marine ecosystems, and coastal regions, promoting informed decision-making for sustainable maritime practices.
Interactive GIS Solutions
MapSwipe City Viewer

MapSwipe City Viewer utilizes ArcGIS’s swipe function to showcase overlays on urban maps. This innovative tool allows users to compare different data layers, such as zoning, demographics, and infrastructure, enhancing spatial awareness and facilitating informed urban planning. By visualizing critical information, we support decision-makers in promoting sustainable development.
